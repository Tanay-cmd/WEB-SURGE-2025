Master of Computer Applications (MCA)

About

The Master of Computer Applications (MCA) program at IIIT Vadodara will address the growing demand for highly skilled professionals in the field of computer science and information technology. With the advent of transformative technologies such as Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Cloud Computing, and Cybersecurity, the program aims to equip students with cutting-edge technical expertise and analytical skills to excel in dynamic and competitive global markets.

IIIT Vadodara offers programs in Computer Science and IT, supported by research labs and a focus on emerging technologies.

Leveraging IIIT Vadodara's strong foundation in technology education and research, the MCA program will emphasize hands-on learning, industry collaboration, and innovation. The curriculum integrates theoretical knowledge with practical applications, preparing graduates for leadership roles in the IT industry, academia, and entrepreneurship.

Objective

The objectives of the MCA program at IIIT Vadodara are:
1. Advanced Knowledge and Skills: To provide in-depth knowledge in computer science and IT, fostering expertise in software development, system design, and emerging technologies.
2. Problem-Solving and Innovation: To nurture problem-solving and analytical skills, encouraging innovative approaches to address real-world challenges in diverse application domains.
3. Industry Readiness: To bridge the gap between academia and industry through internships, collaborative projects, and exposure to contemporary tools and practices.
4. Research and Development: To inspire students to pursue research and contribute to the development of innovative solutions in computing and allied fields.
5. Ethics and Professionalism: To instill professional ethics, social responsibility, and teamwork, enabling students to contribute meaningfully to society.
6. Capacity Building: To build an ecosystem for sustainable human resource development in the field of IT.
7. Lifelong Learning: To prepare graduates for lifelong learning and adaptability in rapidly evolving technological landscapes.

The MCA program aligns with IIIT Vadodara’s mission to foster excellence in education, research, and innovation in the field of computer science and engineering.

Duration of the Program

Three Years (Six semesters)

Eligibility Criteria

Indian Nationals securing at least 60% marks in aggregate or 6.5 CGPA on a 10-point scale (55% marks or 6.0 CGPA on a 10-point scale in case of SC/ ST/PwD) in
(i) Any UG program of minimum Three years with Mathematics/ Statistics as one of the subjects.
               OR
(ii) B.E./ B.Tech. from a recognized University/Institution are eligible to appear for NIMCET-2024.
Note: Conversion of scored marks from CGPA to percentage and vice-versa is not permitted. CGPA other than 10-point scale will be converted to 10-point scale based on unitary method.

Admission Process

The admission in MCA programme will be based on an all India entrance examination NIMCET score. The admission criterion for MCA will be the same as decided by the NIMCET committee. The institute may conduct SPOT admissions after conclusion of the NIMCET admission process for available seats.
